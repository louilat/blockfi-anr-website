Blockchain and Decentralized Finance – BLOCKFI

Interfaces: Mathematics, Digital Sciences – Humanities and Social Sciences

What about us:

The mathematical modelling of DeFi protocols, the risk assessement of this new ecosystem, the statistical modelling of related data are topics in their infancy within the academic literature. The few available research works are mostly under preprints, submitted for publications, some are cited in the following Work Packages. Nonetheless, the needs for quantitative methods and tools are of paramount importance. Our ambition is to help transform these beginnings of work on these subjects into a real, strong, visible academic research stream.
